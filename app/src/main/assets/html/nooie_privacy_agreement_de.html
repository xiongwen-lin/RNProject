What information does Nooie Cam collect?

 

l  Setup information you provide

When installing Nooie Cam and setting up account, you’ll be asked for certain basic information like your email address and camera name. This information is used to personalize your experience - for example, to tell you which device is triggering a notification.

 

l  Environmental data from the Nooie Cam’s sensor

We collect data from the sensor built into Nooie Cam. The sensor collect data such as device ambient light in the room. By recording this information, Nooie Cam can know whether it’s dark and it should turn on night vision.

 

l  Video and audio signals and data

Nooie Cam can help you determine the purpose and the means for which you collect video and audio signals data. If you set up your camera to receive mobile notifications, we may process information from your Nooie Cam to send you alerts when something happens. In addition, if you have the recording features enabled, we will capture, process and retain video and audio data recordings from your device for the duration of your recording subscription period (for example, 7, 14 or 30 days) and you will be able to access those recordings using the Services during that time.

 

l  Technical information from the device

In order to improve your experience over time and help troubleshoot any problem you may encounter with Nooie Cam, we record your Nooie Cam model and serial number, software version, and technical information.

 

l  Shared content

We will collect and store information that will allow us to save or share your content at your instruction through the Services.

What additional information does Nooie collect and store when a user connects a Nooie product to the Internet or creates an account?

 

Wi-Fi Network Information: To connect the Nooie product to our Services or to access Nooie products from your smartphone or tablet over the Internet, you need to connect them to a Wi-Fi network. During the installation process, the Nooie product will require you to provide a Wi-Fi network name (SSID) and password to connect to the Internet. It saves this information along with your IP address on the device so that you can access and control it from your smartphone or tablet, and so that it can communicate with the Nooie server and download software updates.

 

Other Authorized Users: Nooie allows you to enable other authorized users to access your camera. If you do this, other authorized users can view the content from your Nooie Cam. Other authorized users must own a Nooie account.

 

Basic Information: Your account allows you to provide certain basic information such as your name and profile photo. The name and profile photo may be displayed to others related to the service. In addition, if you download the Nooie Cam App, we may collect and store your mobile device identifier (such as IDFA, AAID or other device identifier). For example, if you invite someone to be another authorized user, he or she will be able to see your name and photo. You should only use photos that you are willing to disclose.

Enhance your Smart Home experience with Amazon Alexa and control the devices using your voice. How to connect with Alexa? Osaio Skill must be enabled in the Alexa App. Open Alexa App and tap Skills in the menu. From the search bar, type Osaio and enable the skill. How to control Osaio via Alexa? Please set an easily recognizable name for your device. Once the Osaio Skill is enabled in Alexa app, simply ask Alexa to turn on or turn off device by using the device name saved in Osaio App. For example: “Alexa,turn (name of device) on." 
"Alexa, turn (name of device) off."

Enhance your Smart Home experience with Google Assistant and control the devices using your voice. How to connect with Google Assistant? Osaio Skill must be enabled in the Google Home App. Open Google Home App and tap the plus sign Add in the main page. Select Set Up Device and choose the Have Something Already Set Up? under the Works with Google. Search for Osaio in the list and open it, confirm the country where your Osaio account is located, enter your Osaio account and password. Tap Authorize to complete. How to control Osaio via Google Assistant? Please set an easily recognizable name for your device. Once the Osaio Skill is enabled in Google Home app, simply give a commend to Google Assistant to show or stop device live video using the device name saved in Osaio App. For example: "Hey Google (or OK Google),show (name of device or group)."
"Hey Google (or OK Google), stop (name of device or group)."
